Exploring our city. On foot.

In January 2023, we set out to experience Vancouver in a new way. Every Sunday, rain or shine, we plan and walk a 10+ km route that weaves through neighborhoods, parks, streets and trails.

It has been over three years and we're still at it! Why? Because we love getting outside, having long chats and finding local gems like eateries and scenic viewpoints!

Check out our journey, and maybe find inspiration to explore your surroundings. Whether you’re a longtime resident or a visitor, these walks reveal a side of the city that’s off the beaten path!
